Community Bulletin

The City Council has announced several community development initiatives aimed at improving public spaces and encouraging citizen participation. Residents are invited to attend monthly meetings to discuss local concerns and propose new projects.

A large-scale tree plantation drive is scheduled for next month. Volunteers from schools, colleges, and neighborhood associations will participate in planting hundreds of saplings across parks and roadside areas.

The annual Food and Culture Festival attracted thousands of visitors this year. The event featured traditional performances, local cuisine, and exhibitions from community organizations.

New recreational facilities, including walking tracks and outdoor fitness zones, are also being planned. These projects are expected to enhance public health and strengthen community engagement throughout the city.
